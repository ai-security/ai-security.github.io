Awarded Special Prize at 2013 National Cryptography Contest

The team (member: Inho Hahn, Wontae Jang) in the Lab was awarded Special Prize at 2013 Korea Cryptography Contest on November 14, 2013. Korea Cryptography Contest is one of prestigious contests which are organized by both KISA (Korea Internet & Security Agency) and NSRI (National Security Research Institute), and sponsored by both NIS (National Intelligence Service) and MSIP (Ministry of Science, ICT and Future Planning). Participating in the contest for the first time, the Lab showed its potential in regard to information security. The contest mainly consists of two areas, problem solving based on cryptanalysis/cyber security techniques and research papers for information security. The team participated in the first area. The team members have been advised under both Prof. Seong Oun Hwang and Intae Kim, the Lab leader.
