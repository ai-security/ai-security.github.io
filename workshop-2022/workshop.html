National AI Strategy

International Workshop 2022

6-7th December 2022

 

 

Theme: National AI Strategy

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the fastest growing deep technology in the world, with huge potential to change entire industries, drive substantial economic growth and transform all areas of life. Israel and Korea are global superpowers in AI and are well placed to be the world leaders over the next decade as a genuine research and innovation powerhouse, hub of talent and business environment. In this workshop, we will review Israeli and Korean national AI strategy, and discuss the Israeli and Korean policy for investment and planning for the long-term needs of the AI ecosystem to build and maintain leadership in AI science and industry. We will examine what is needed to encourage national innovation, growth, expertise, industrial leadership, investment, and protect the public and our fundamental values.

Speaker

Bio

Dr. Orna Berry

Orna Berry is an Israeli computer scientist, high-tech entrepreneur, and senior executive in the Israeli science and technology industries. In 1996, Berry became the first woman to serve as chief scientist and head of the industrial R&D operation of the Israeli Ministry of Industry, Trade and Labour. She was awarded the "Yekirat Hanegev" award from Ben-Gurion University of the Negev in 2012.

Currently, Berry serves as director of technology in the office of the CTO at Google Cloud.

She was among the committee who wrote the Israeli AI National Strategy

Dr. Ziv Katzir

Dr. Katzir is the head of the National Program for AI Infrastructure @ Israel Innovation Authority

Ziv heads the Israel’s National Program for AI Infrastructure, a coordinated, collaborative government effort aimed at ensuring Israel’s future leadership in the global AI arena. The program is focused on facilitating long-term infrastructure for sustained innovation and growth, initially targeting the establishment of an Israeli High-Performance Computer; Creation of infrastructure for Sematic language processing; Increasing academic human capital and removal of regulatory barriers.

Ziv is a versatile technologist with profound knowledge of Artificial Intelligence, an experienced innovation leader, and growth-oriented CTO.

Mr. Seffi Cohen

LTC Seffi Cohen is an AI researcher. He received a B.Sc degree in computer science from MTA, Tel-Aviv, in 2010 and an M.Sc degree in computer science from the Open University, Israel, in 2018. He is currently studying for a Ph.D. in software and information systems engineering at Ben Gurion University.

He founded the first data science team in the Israeli Defense Force, which became the AI COE. This COE led dozens of various AI solutions successfully deployed and yielded tangible operational value. Seffi is a Kaggle master and AI mentor at Google For Startups. He has mentored hundreds of startups, participated in many international AI competitions, and reached the top of the leaderboard in many of them.

Prof. Lior Rokach

Lior Rokach is a professor of Software and Information Systems Engineering (SISE) at

Ben-Gurion University of the Negev (BGU). His research interests lie in the design, development, and analysis of Machine Learning and Data Mining algorithms and their applications in Recommender Systems, Cyber Security and Medical Informatics. Prof. Rokach is the author of over 300 peer-reviewed papers in leading journals and conference proceedings. He is also the author of six books and the co-editor of three books. Rokach has co-founded four AI companies and has been awarded 22 patents for his inventions in AI and information technology.

Dr. Bracha Shapira

Prof. Bracha Shapira is a Professor at the Department of Software and Information Systems Engineering.  She served as the head of department for 6 years and as the vice dean for research at the faculty of engineering sciences. Bracha’s research is in the field of  machine learning (ML). She applies to various domains, mainly recommender systems, personalization , challenges, and medical informatics. She leads a group of ~20 research students and collaborates with various industry partners and government entities. She published about 200 publications in journals, conferences, and books. She also has industry experience in various consultant and management roles in AI based companies and was awarded 12 patents in the field of AI.

Dr. Myung-kyu Yi

MYUNG-KYU YI received a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Korea University in 2005. He is currently a Professor with Gachon University. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, and human activity recognition.

Dr. Abdul Majeed

Abdul Majeed received a Ph.D. in Computer Information Systems & Networks from Korea Aerospace University in 2021. He is currently an Assistant Professor with Gachon University. His research interests include Data-centric AI, data mining, machine learning, and privacy-aware computing.
