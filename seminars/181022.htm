전문가초청세미나

  제목: Introduction to Post-Quantum Cryptography

  연사: 조선대학교 김영식 교수님

  일시: 2018년 10월 22일 (월)

  장소: 홍익대학교 소프트웨어융합학과 D310-1

  내용:

In this talk, I introduce the recent trend of the post-quantum cryptography (PQC) algorithms that have been actively researched recently. In addition, I will introduce the PQC standardization by the NIST of United States and explain the basic principles of code-based cryptography and lattice-based cryptography actively discussed in the standardization process. In code-based cryptosystems, cryptographic algorithms using both the Hamming metric-based codes and rank metric-based codes have been proposed. Using some representative algorithms, I will explain the main structures of those algorithms. On the other hand, most of the lattice-based cryptosystems are designed based on the learning with errors (LWE) problem and the main structures of individual algorithms are substantially identical with small differences. Therefore, the basic design principle of each algorithm will be explained based on the original LWE problem.
