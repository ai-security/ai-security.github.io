Grand Prize Awarded at 2018 Graduation Exhibition Contest

Sangjun Yoon, Seunghan Lee and Hoyoung Kim in the Lab were awarded Grand Prize at 2018 Graduation Exhibition Contest on November 20, 2018. Graduation Exhibition Contest is annually organized by Student Association for Department of Software and Communications Engineering and sponsored by Department of Software and Communications Engineering. In the contest, students compete vigorously with their systems which have been developed based on their four-year learnings and skills. Based on machine learning algorithms which are recent AI technologies, the team developed Firewall System for Automatic Policy Rule Generation based on Artificial Intelligence. This system solved fatal problems of existing firewall systems. They have been advised under both Prof. Seong Oun Hwang and Kyung Hyun Han, the Lab leader.

Admission

We are seeking people including graduates,postdocs, and visitors who are well-prepared to commit their research or practices. Funding including tuition fee, cost of living, and incentive is available to applicants depending upon their performances. To get admission, they should have one of the following language certificates with test scores of at least:

-TOEFL 550 (CBT 210, iBT 80)

-TOEIC 685

-TOPIK (Standard Test of Proficiency in Korean) Level 3

-IELTS 5.5

-TEPS (Test of English Proficiency developed by Seoul National University) 550
