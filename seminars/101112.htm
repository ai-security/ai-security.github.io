  제목: Where does design, art and science converge?

  연사: Daniel Mikesell

  일시: 2010년 11월 12일 (금)

  장소: 홍익아트홀 소극장

  내용:

         Where does design, art and science converge? Join WCU Professor Dan

         Mikesell to explore this question. Professor Mikesell will present his

         work developing the award winning "Hodu : Zero Failure Physical

         Therapy" device and discuss how it relates to information computing,

         interaction design and physical science. He will also talk aboutsome of

         his other award winning work including "Project:Prey" anddiscuss how

         technology influences his work.

  문의: 컴퓨터정보통신공학과 황성운 교수
