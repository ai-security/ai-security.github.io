[I Love You]

A healing music for the people who have a pain in the past love ? ‘I Love You’

The first digital single album ‘I Love You’ was released by the artist Maybrill on March 19, 2018. This album contains feelings such as sorrow and loneliness which lovers suffer, and expresses the whole heart after farewell.

In this album, Hyojin Jeon, a vocalist of the female duo named ‘add2’, expressed the song in her unique sorrowful and sweet tone. We hope that a soft acoustic piano melody harmonized with her voice conveys a warm comfort to the listeners.

You can enjoy 'I Love You' by Maybrill at Melon, Genie Music, Apple Music.
